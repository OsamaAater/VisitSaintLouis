Attractions

The Gateway Arch

The Gateway Arch located in Downtown St. Louis, MO stands an astonishing 630ft tall. It is equipped with a tram system that will take visitors to the top of the Arch where you can look out small windows and see the city for miles.

The St. Louis Science Center

The Saint Louis Science Center located in the infamous Forest Park. It features over 750 exhibits such as, a Dinosaur exhibit, a planetarium, and a 5-story domed OMNIMAX theater. There are 2 buildings that connect by an enclosed bridge over Interstate 40.

The St. Louis Zoo

With over 600 species and 18,700 animals on 90 acres of land. The Saint Louis Zoo is a popular attraction for visitors of all ages. There is no entrance fee to visit.

Busch Stadium

This is a multi-purpose sports facility, best known for Home of the St. Louis Cardinals MLB team. It can accommodate over 50, 000 fans for any event. Located inside Busch Stadium is a dining and entertainment district, Ballpark Village. It’s fun to visit even if there is no event.
